Soal Determinan Matriks Dan Adjoin#

A. Hitunglah determinan matrik berikut dengan menggunakan rumus expansi baris#

\[ \det(A)=\sum_{k=1}^{n}(-1)^{i+k}a_{ik}M_{ik} \]

dengan ( M_{ij} ) adalah minor dari matriks ( A ), dan

\[ M_{ij}=\det(A_{ij}) \]

dengan ( A_{ij} ) adalah submatriks yang diperoleh dengan menghapus baris ke-(i) dan kolom ke-(j) dari matriks ( A_{m \times n} ), untuk

\[ 1 \le i,j \le n \]

1.#

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5\\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

2.#

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3\\ 1 & -2 & -1\\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

3.#

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1\\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

B. Gunakan rumus matriks adjoin untuk menghitung invers dari matriks berikut#

\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)_{ij}=(-1)^{i+j}M_{ji} \]

dan

\[ A^{-1}=\frac{1}{\det(A)}\operatorname{adj}(A) \]

4.#

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5\\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

5.#

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3\\ 1 & -2 & -1\\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

6.#

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1\\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Jawaban#

Penyelesaian Soal Nomor A1#

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5\\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Determinan matriks 2×2:

\[ \det(A)=ad-bc \]
\[ \det(A)=(-7)(4)-(-5)(1) \]
\[ =-28+5 \]
\[ =-23 \]

Jadi,

\[ \boxed{\det(A)=-23} \]

Penyelesaian Soal Nomor A2#

Diketahui matriks: $\(A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\)$

Langkah-langkah Penyelesaian (Ekspansi Baris Ketiga):

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3\\ 1 & -2 & -1\\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Ekspansi baris pertama:

\[ \det(A)=0\cdot M_{11}-2\cdot M_{12}+(-3)\cdot M_{13} \]

Karena elemen pertama bernilai nol, maka:

\[ \det(A)=-2M_{12}-3M_{13} \]

Minor (M_{12})

\[\begin{split} M_{12}= \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -1\\ 0 & 1 \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[ =(1)(1)-(-1)(0) \]
\[ =1 \]

Minor (M_{13})

\[\begin{split} M_{13}= \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -2\\ 0 & 0 \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[ =(1)(0)-(-2)(0) \]
\[ =0 \]

Substitusi:

\[ \det(A)=-2(1)-3(0) \]
\[ =-2 \]

Jadi,

\[ \boxed{\det(A)=-2} \]

Penyelesaian Nomor A3: Determinan Matriks 4x4#

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1\\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Ekspansi baris pertama:

\[ \det(A)=1M_{11}-(-3)M_{12}+1M_{13}-1M_{14} \]

Minor (M_{11})

\[\begin{split} M_{11}= \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & -3 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} =1 \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1\\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} -1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} +1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3\\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[ =1(9-1)-1(-3-1)+1(1+3) \]
\[ =8+4+4=16 \]

Minor (M_{12})

\[\begin{split} M_{12}= \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & -3 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} =(-3) \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1\\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} -1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} +1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3\\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[ =(-3)(9-1)-1(-3-1)+1(1+3) \]
\[ =-24+4+4=-16 \]

Minor (M_{13})

\[\begin{split} M_{13}= \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} =(-3) \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} -1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} +1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[ =(-3)(-4)-1(-4)+1(0) \]
\[ =12+4=16 \]

Minor (M_{14})

\[\begin{split} M_{14}= \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3\\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} =(-3) \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3\\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} -1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3\\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} +1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[ =(-3)(4)-1(4)+0 \]
\[ =-12-4=-16 \]

Substitusi:

\[ \det(A)=1(16)-(-3)(-16)+1(16)-1(-16) \]
\[ =16-48+16+16 \]
\[ =0 \]

Jadi,

\[ \boxed{\det(A)=0} \]

Penyelesaian Soal Nomor B4#

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5\\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Determinan matriks:

\[ \det(A)=(-7)(4)-(-5)(1) \]
\[ =-28+5=-23 \]

Rumus invers matriks:

\[ A^{-1}=\frac{1}{\det(A)}\operatorname{adj}(A) \]

  1. Mencari adjoin matriks

Untuk matriks 2×2:

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} a & b\\ c & d \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

maka:

\[\begin{split} \operatorname{adj}(A)= \begin{bmatrix} d & -b\\ -c & a \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Sehingga:

\[\begin{split} \operatorname{adj}(A)= \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5\\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

  1. Mencari invers matriks

\[\begin{split} A^{-1}=\frac{1}{-23} \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5\\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} A^{-1}= \begin{bmatrix} -\frac{4}{23} & -\frac{5}{23}\\ \frac{1}{23} & \frac{7}{23} \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Jadi,

\[\begin{split} \boxed{ A^{-1}= \begin{bmatrix} -\frac{4}{23} & -\frac{5}{23}\\ \frac{1}{23} & \frac{7}{23} \end{bmatrix} } \end{split}\]

Penyelesaian Soal Nomor B5#

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3\\ 1 & -2 & -1\\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Dari hasil sebelumnya diperoleh:

\[ \det(A)=-2 \]

Rumus invers matriks:

\[ A^{-1}=\frac{1}{\det(A)}\operatorname{adj}(A) \]

  1. Mencari matriks kofaktor

Kofaktor (C_{11})

\[\begin{split} C_{11}=(-1)^{1+1} \begin{vmatrix} -2 & -1\\ 0 & 1 \end{vmatrix} =-2 \end{split}\]

Kofaktor (C_{12})

\[\begin{split} C_{12}=(-1)^{1+2} \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -1\\ 0 & 1 \end{vmatrix} =-1 \end{split}\]

Kofaktor (C_{13})

\[\begin{split} C_{13}=(-1)^{1+3} \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -2\\ 0 & 0 \end{vmatrix} =0 \end{split}\]

Kofaktor (C_{21})

\[\begin{split} C_{21}=(-1)^{2+1} \begin{vmatrix} 2 & -3\\ 0 & 1 \end{vmatrix} =-2 \end{split}\]

Kofaktor (C_{22})

\[\begin{split} C_{22}=(-1)^{2+2} \begin{vmatrix} 0 & -3\\ 0 & 1 \end{vmatrix} =0 \end{split}\]

Kofaktor (C_{23})

\[\begin{split} C_{23}=(-1)^{2+3} \begin{vmatrix} 0 & 2\\ 0 & 0 \end{vmatrix} =0 \end{split}\]

Kofaktor (C_{31})

\[\begin{split} C_{31}=(-1)^{3+1} \begin{vmatrix} 2 & -3\\ -2 & -1 \end{vmatrix} =-8 \end{split}\]

Kofaktor (C_{32})

\[\begin{split} C_{32}=(-1)^{3+2} \begin{vmatrix} 0 & -3\\ 1 & -1 \end{vmatrix} =-3 \end{split}\]

Kofaktor (C_{33})

\[\begin{split} C_{33}=(-1)^{3+3} \begin{vmatrix} 0 & 2\\ 1 & -2 \end{vmatrix} =-2 \end{split}\]

Maka matriks kofaktor:

\[\begin{split} C= \begin{bmatrix} -2 & -1 & 0\\ -2 & 0 & 0\\ -8 & -3 & -2 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

  1. Mencari adjoin

Transpose matriks kofaktor:

\[\begin{split} \operatorname{adj}(A)=C^T= \begin{bmatrix} -2 & -2 & -8\\ -1 & 0 & -3\\ 0 & 0 & -2 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

  1. Mencari invers matriks

\[\begin{split} A^{-1}=\frac{1}{-2} \begin{bmatrix} -2 & -2 & -8\\ -1 & 0 & -3\\ 0 & 0 & -2 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} A^{-1}= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 4\\ \frac{1}{2} & 0 & \frac{3}{2}\\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Jadi,

\[\begin{split} \boxed{ A^{-1}= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 4\\ \frac{1}{2} & 0 & \frac{3}{2}\\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} } \end{split}\]

Penyelesaian Detail Nomor 6: Invers Matriks 4x4

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1\\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Dari hasil sebelumnya diperoleh:

\[ \det(A)=0 \]

Rumus invers matriks dengan adjoin:

\[ A^{-1}=\frac{1}{\det(A)}\operatorname{adj}(A) \]

Substitusi:

\[ A^{-1}=\frac{1}{0}\operatorname{adj}(A) \]

Karena pembagian dengan nol tidak terdefinisi, maka matriks (A) tidak memiliki invers.

\[ \boxed{A^{-1}\text{ tidak ada}} \]

Penyelesaian Soal Nomor B6#

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1\\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1\\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Dari hasil perhitungan determinan sebelumnya diperoleh:

\[ \det(A)=0 \]

Rumus invers matriks:

\[ A^{-1}=\frac{1}{\det(A)}\operatorname{adj}(A) \]

Substitusi:

\[ A^{-1}=\frac{1}{0}\operatorname{adj}(A) \]

Karena pembagian dengan nol tidak terdefinisi, maka matriks (A) tidak memiliki invers.

\[ \boxed{A^{-1}\text{ tidak ada}} \]
